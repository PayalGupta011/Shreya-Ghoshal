Shreya Ghoshal

Female Singer

Shreya Ghoshal is an acclaimed Indian playback singer known for her versatile voice, winning numerous awards like five National Film Awards and seven Filmfare Awards. She began her career after winning the reality show Sa Re Ga Ma Pa at age 16, which led to her debut in the film Devdas. With over 3,000 songs in 20+ languages, she is a globally recognized artist who has also worked on independent music projects.
